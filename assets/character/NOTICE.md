## Shin-chan Character Peek Assets

- Peek images are local derived transparent PNGs generated from a KindPNG image for local personal-use testing.
- Source page for the derived character image: https://www.kindpng.com/imgv/hToJJRb_crayon-shin-chan-png-transparent-png/
- The page labels the image license as "Personal Use Only".
- `peek_left.png`, `peek_right.png`, `peek_top.png`, and `peek_bottom.png` are local derived transparent PNGs generated by `scripts/generate_peek_assets.py`.
